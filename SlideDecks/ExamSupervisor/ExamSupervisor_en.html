Setting up and testing the Safe Exam Browser (SEB)


  • Go to https://certification.joomla.org/faq scroll to “System Requirements” part and download the “Safe Exam Browser” for your Operation System.

  • Start the “Safe Exam Browser” to see if it doesn’t give any errors.

  • We will distribute the exam start-up file via USB stick.

  • Tell us in which language you want to do the exam. We will then enrol you in the exam language chosen.
